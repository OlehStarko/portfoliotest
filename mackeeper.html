Challenge

The MacKeeper brand has been on the market for quite a long time and has established itself as a stable and reliable Mac care tool. But, surprisingly, people began to associate the logo symbol with the character of the famous cult film “Star Wars” (Java). This fact, as it turns out, reduces trust in the brand and does not reveal the main value - real human technical support inside the product along with all its functions. Therefore, the decision was made to rethink the visual identity and make the logo more friendly, trustworthy, positive and innovative.

Premium Services

Along with the MacBook care application, MacKeeper has an incredible technical support department with knowledgeable experts who can solve any technical issues related to devices. Accordingly, the logo of this service should be clearly readable as a child product of the McKeeper, so in the process of searching for options, we decided to settle on the connecting element of the name ligature with a smile and change the basic form of the application to a communication bubble.
